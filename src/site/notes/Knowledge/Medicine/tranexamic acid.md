# tranexamic acid
- synthetic derivative of lysine
- blocks lysine binding sites of [plasminogen](plasminogen) molecules
	- inhibit interaction of [plasminogen](plasminogen) w/ [[plasmin\|plasmin]] & [[fibrin\|fibrin]]
- ADR
	- thrombotic events
	- post-op seizures
